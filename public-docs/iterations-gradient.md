# Iteration Gradient

Maps the number of iterations before a point escapes to colors in your gradient.

Points that escape quickly get early colors; points that take more iterations get later colors. This creates the characteristic colored bands that make fractals visually interesting.

Adjust your gradient to create different visual effects - linear progression for smooth transitions, non-linear steps for dramatic color changes, or clustered colors to emphasize specific iteration ranges.
