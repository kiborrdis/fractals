# Mirroring & Symmetry

Creates symmetric patterns by mirroring the fractal across different axes or arrangements.

**Options:**
- **Off**: No mirroring
- **Square**: Mirror across horizontal/vertical axes
- **Hex**: Hexagonal/6-fold symmetry
- **Radial**: Circular symmetry with customizable angle

Mirroring creates kaleidoscope-like effects and can reveal hidden patterns in the fractal.
